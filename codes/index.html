Relay Task
(The relay programming learner is expected to carry out the following task which will tell whether the programmer 
 proceeds to the next programming stage or not. In addition he/she is supposed to have solved all the tasks he/she had 
 been issued with by his/her trainer. Upon completion of the following task, he/she is supposed to return the 
 completed circuit to his/her trainer who will determine whether the circuit contains errors or not. If not 
 the learner shall have qualified to proceed to the next programming stage i.e. Flood2.)

Task
Develop a well-designed circuit for a device that has the following capabilities: Has a button for switching on its beam 
light and another button b for switching on the flood light. Has button c for switching on the propeller and finally 
button d for switching off anything on. (All the buttons should be single tap). The device is rechargeable and has LEDâ€™s 
to indicate that it is charging. Upon pressing button d to switch off anything that is on, the lights should first delay 
then shrink off. This device is 12volts DC operated; the LED indicators are 5volts DC operated and the lights and the 
propeller are also 12volts DC operated.
